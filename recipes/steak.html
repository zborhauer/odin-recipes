Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • herbs

Steps

  1. put oil in pan, get it searing hot
  2. season steak
  3. put steak in pan
  4. cook it on one side
  5. flip steak, add butter to pan
  6. spoon butter on to the steak from the pan
  7. pull steak after 5 minutes and let rest
  8. enjoy beautifuly!
